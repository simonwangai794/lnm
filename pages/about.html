Our Story

Founded in 2018, EarnConnect has been at the forefront of digital innovation in Kenya, creating platforms that empower individuals to earn income through technology while providing affordable connectivity solutions.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in Kenya by providing accessible earning opportunities and affordable connectivity solutions to all Kenyans.

Our Journey

2018

EarnConnect was founded in Nairobi with a vision to create digital earning opportunities for Kenyans.

2019

Launched our first digital earning platform, helping over 10,000 Kenyans generate income online.

2021

Partnered with JTL to introduce Faiba SIM services, revolutionizing affordable data access across Kenya.

2022

Expanded our services to include M-Pesa integration, creating a seamless ecosystem for earning and payments.

2024

Reached 100,000 active users across all platforms, with over Ksh 500 million in earnings generated for our community.

Empowering Digital Kenya

Income Opportunities

Our platforms create multiple streams of income for Kenyans, from watching and sharing videos to promoting products and services. We've designed our earning systems to be accessible to everyone, regardless of education level or technical expertise.

Affordable Connectivity

Through our partnership with JTL, we offer Faiba SIM cards with the most affordable data bundles in Kenya. Our no-expiry policy ensures that users never lose their data, making internet access more equitable and accessible.

Digital Skills Training

We provide free digital literacy training to help Kenyans maximize their earning potential. Our workshops cover everything from basic smartphone usage to advanced online marketing techniques, empowering users with valuable skills.

Community Support

Our community forums and support groups connect users across Kenya, creating networks for knowledge sharing and collaboration. We regularly organize meetups in different counties to strengthen these connections and gather feedback.
