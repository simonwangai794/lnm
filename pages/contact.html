Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

EarnConnect offers multiple ways to earn money, including watching and sharing videos, completing surveys, referring friends, and promoting products. After signing up, you can access all earning opportunities through your dashboard and choose the ones that work best for you.

You can get a Faiba SIM card by visiting our office in Westlands, ordering online through our website, or contacting our customer service team. We also have distribution partners across major towns in Kenya where you can purchase a SIM card. Each SIM card costs Ksh 100 and comes with Ksh 50 worth of data.

Payments are processed every Friday for all earnings accumulated during the week. You can choose to receive your payments via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or as airtime/data bundles. The minimum withdrawal amount is Ksh 500, and there are no withdrawal fees when using M-Pesa.

Faiba data bundles offer several unique advantages: they don't expire (no-expiry policy), they're significantly more affordable than other providers, they provide faster 4G speeds in covered areas, and they can be shared with friends and family through our app.

For technical issues or payment problems, you can contact our support team through the contact form on this page, via WhatsApp, or by calling our customer service line. Please provide details of the issue, including screenshots if possible, to help us resolve your problem quickly.
