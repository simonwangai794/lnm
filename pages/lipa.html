Getting Started Guide

Everything you need to know to start earning cashback on your M-Pesa payments

Requirements for Linking M-Pesa

  • An active M-Pesa account registered in your name

  • A smartphone with the EarnConnect app installed

  • Valid identification (ID card or passport)

  • Access to the phone number registered with your M-Pesa account

Security Information

End-to-End Encryption

All your transaction data is encrypted and securely stored. We use bank-level security protocols to protect your information.

Limited Access

We only have read-only access to your transaction history. We cannot initiate transactions or access your M-Pesa balance.

Control Your Data

You can disconnect your M-Pesa account at any time. All your transaction data will be deleted from our servers upon request.

Terms and Conditions

• Cashback rates are subject to change with 30 days notice

• Minimum withdrawal amount is KSh 100

• Maximum monthly cashback is KSh 10,000

• Cashback is calculated and credited within 24 hours of transaction

FAQ

How long does it take to receive cashback?

Cashback is typically credited to your account within 24 hours of the transaction being processed.

Is there a fee to join the program?

No, joining the M-Pesa cashback program is completely free.

Can I link multiple M-Pesa accounts?

Currently, you can only link one M-Pesa account per EarnConnect profile.

How do I withdraw my cashback?

Once you've accumulated at least KSh 100, you can withdraw your cashback directly to your M-Pesa account through the app.

Which transactions don't qualify for cashback?

Person-to-person transfers, betting/gambling payments, and some government services don't qualify for cashback.
