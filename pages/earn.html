Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our video earning platform

Earnings vary based on the video type and length. On average, you can earn between KSh 10-35 per video. Educational content and surveys typically offer higher rates than entertainment videos. The more videos you watch, the more you earn.

The minimum withdrawal amount is KSh 500. Once you reach this threshold, you can transfer your earnings directly to your M-Pesa account. Withdrawals are processed instantly during business hours and within 24 hours during weekends and holidays.

We add new videos daily across all categories. On average, there are 30-50 new videos available each day. The exact number varies based on your profile, preferences, and the campaigns currently running. Premium members get access to more videos.

You need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a stable internet connection. Our platform works on all modern browsers. For the best experience, we recommend a connection speed of at least 1 Mbps. Videos can be watched in standard definition to save on data usage.

After watching each video, you'll need to answer 1-3 simple verification questions to prove you've watched the content. These questions are straightforward if you've paid attention to the video. You need to answer correctly to earn points for that video.
